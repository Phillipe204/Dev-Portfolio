## Design Direction

The final website should feel like a mix of:

* **Apple**
* **modern SaaS landing page**
* **premium developer portfolio**
* **clean tech brand**

Avoid:

* clutter
* overly flashy neon effects
* too many colors
* excessive motion
* cheap-looking gradients
* bulky layouts

Prefer:

* dark background with subtle lighting/glow
* elegant blue/white/soft gray accent palette
* lots of breathing room
* big clean headings
* polished buttons
* refined cards
* minimal but beautiful design
